Pizza

Description

Make the best homemade Italian pizza dough recipe - All you need is 5 simple ingredients for the best traditional pizza with a thin crispy crust! This super easy pizza dough recipe follows the traditional Neapolitan version, and believe me, it is absolutely incredible. My Italian dad was a pizzaiolo for more than 30 years, so I grew up eating lots of pizza. Over the years, I managed to learn all his secrets for the perfect pizza and enjoy every single bite of it.

Ingredients

  • water
  • flour
  • yeast
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Place lukewarm (do not use hot water!) water in a small bowl, sprinkle the yeast and a pinch of sugar on top, mix and let it rest for 5-10 minutes, until the mixture is nice and bubbly.
  2. Then pour the mixture into the mixer, add olive oil and slowly incorporate the flour.
  3. The dough comes together after 5-6 minutes of mixing time.
  4. Work the ball of dough with your hands on a clean flour-dusted surface until smooth and even.
  5. Transfer the ball in a lightly oiled bowl and cover with cling film or a damp kitchen towel.
  6. Allow it to rest in a warm place (about 24-27Â°C) for about 2-3 hours until it has doubled in size.
  7. After it has rested, divide the dough into 2-3 smaller pieces and form smooth and even balls.
  8. Then transfer into a lightly oiled baking sheet and cover with a slightly damp kitchen towel.
  9. Allow the balls to rest and raise for an extra 3-4 hours.
  10. Preheat your oven to 250C-280C / 480F- 530F.
  11. Instead of using a rolling pin, spread each ball of dough with your hands to form a large circle.
  12. For a classic pizza Margherita, spread just enough marinara sauce (aka pizza sauce) on top, leaving a border.
  13. Place it in the oven and cook it for about 3 minutes, then add sliced mozzarella (make sure to pat the slices dry with kitchen paper) and cook for a further 1-2 minutes.
  14. Remove the pizza from the oven, scatter a few basil leaves on top and serve.
